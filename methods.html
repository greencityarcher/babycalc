    Methods of predicting the gender of the child

    The BabyCalc prediction method uses the theory of blood renewal, the Chinese baby chart, and the Japanese pregnancy calendar. Thanks to the integrated use of the three theories together, BabyCalc allows you to get a more accurate result compared to calculations using individual methods.

    What calculation methods does this site use?

    Blood renewal method

    Since the methods for predicting gender are not based on accurate medical research and were created independently of each other, in some cases the results of their calculation do not match.

    One of the simplest methods for predicting the gender of a child is by the "age" of blood. It is assumed that in men, a complete renewal of the blood composition occurs every 4 years. And for women - every 3 years. To find out the "age of blood", it is necessary to divide the age of the father by 4, and the age of the mother by 3. Discarding the integer part, we obtain the "age of blood" of each of the parents. In other words, the number of full update cycles is not taken into account. If the mother’s blood is "younger", that is, updated later than her father’s blood, there will be a girl. If the father’s blood is "younger", a boy will be born.

    Large blood loss can shift the natural cycle of blood renewal. If one of the parents has undergone surgery, donation or blood transfusion, then instead of the date of birth, the date of the last blood loss should be indicated. This will be the start of the blood renewal cycle.

    Analysis of reviews of this methodology on forums for pregnant women shows that the accuracy of such a forecast is approximately 62%.

    This gender prediction technique is especially popular among expectant mothers in Russia, Ukraine and Belarus.

    Chinese lunar calendar

    In China, the lunar calendar method for predicting the gender of the child has become widespread. This method is based on an ancient table compiled more than 1000 years ago. The table is calculated for mother's age from 18 to 45 years. In it, for each age and month in which the conception occurred, the the gender of the child is indicated. Father's age is not used for calculation.

    This table is easy to find on the Internet. However, it should be borne in mind that for the correct calculation, you must specify the age and month of conception according to the Chinese lunar calendar. The age and date of conception on the lunar calendar and the European do not match. This is due to the fact that according to the lunar calendar, the age at the time of birth is already 1 year, and is added not on the day of birth, but on the national holiday of Spring or Chinese New Year.

    BabyCalc will automatically translate your dates into the lunar calendar and perform the correct calculation.

    This method is used by many Chinese families who want a child of a certain gender.

    Japanese table

    The Japanese method for predicting the gender of the child is similar to the Chinese, but it also takes into account the date of birth of the father. It includes 2 tables. In the first table for each pair, "father’s month of birth - mother’s month of birth" contains a number. In the second table, for each number from the first table and the calendar month, the probability of conceiving a boy or girl is indicated. Thus, the Japanese method allows you to plan the gender of the child for any period of time.

    Finding Japanese baby charts on the Internet is easy. To use them, you do not need to translate dates into the lunar calendar.

    Surveys show that the accuracy of the Japanese method is 70-80%.
