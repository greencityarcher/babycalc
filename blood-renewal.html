    Gender of a child for blood renewal

    The determination of a child’s gender by blood is based on the assumption that the blood, mucous membranes and other tissues of a person are completely updated in the process of life with a certain frequency. For men, the period of complete renewal of blood and tissues lasts 4 years, and for women - 3 years. If at the time of conception the blood of a woman is "younger" than the blood of a man, a girl will be born. If the blood of a man has been updated recently, and women - a long time ago, then a boy will be born. In other words, the child will have the gender of the parent whose blood is "younger".

    How to calculate the sex of the child for blood renewal?

    1. Divide the father’s age by 4, and the mother’s age by 3.
    2. Discard the whole parts from the received numbers (the number of complete cycles does not affect the "age" of the blood) and compare the residues.
    3. Who has a smaller balance, he has a more "young" blood, therefore this gender will be a child.

    When determining the sex of the child by blood, it should be borne in mind that if one or both parents suffered major blood loss in the past, instead of the date of birth, the date of blood loss should be used in the calculations. Examples of situations when a major blood loss occurs - serious injuries, blood transfusion, blood donation, childbirth, serious surgery.
