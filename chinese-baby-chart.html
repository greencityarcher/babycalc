    Chinese baby chart

    The Chinese table for determining the sex of the child by the age of the mother is known for more than 700 years. Its original has been preserved and is still stored at the Beijing Institute. Recognized in China, the baby gender calendar is now popular all over the world. It is used both to find out the sex of the child from the table, and in order to choose the most successful day to conceive a boy or girl.

    Despite the fact that the Chinese table of the sex of the child is not based on medical or scientific theories, more than 75% of mothers note its reliability.

    How to determine the sex of the child by the date of conception?

    To find out the sex of the child from the table, you must first calculate the lunar age of the mother. The fact is that the Chinese calendar for determining the sex of the child is built on the basis of the Chinese lunar calendar. According to him, the mother’s age will be a year or two more than the age according to the European calendar.

    You can find out your lunar age online using the calculator on this page.

    Knowing your lunar age and month of conception, it remains only to find the appropriate cell in the table, it will indicate the most likely gender of the child.
